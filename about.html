About Us


For over two decades, VhoTel has been graciously inviting guests to breathtaking destinations in the most captivating manner. Together, we have embarked on explorations, uncovering new destinations, endless possibilities, and boundless horizons. Representing the pinnacle of opulence, our brand is deeply committed to curating and designing immersive, significant, and unforgettable experiences for each and every guest.

Our Story


Since 1999, across decades and continents, innovation and discovery has always guided the evolution of the VhoTel brand.

Strategy


As a luxury hotel company, we take immense pride in our origins and are driven by our unwavering dedication to providing every guest with a truly memorable experience. Our carefully crafted long-term approach ensures that we embrace innovation conscientiously and expand selectively, allowing our success story to thrive and our collection of hotels to radiate even more brilliantly.
